  <Card

## Revenue Sources

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transaction Fees" icon="money-bill">
    - **Usage Fees**: Charged when agents complete actions like executing trades or rebalancing assets
    - **Commission Splits**: Portion of referral fees earned from other protocols
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Subscription Plans" icon="calendar">
    High-value AI agents or advanced analytics services run on a subscription model, with a portion of payments going to stakers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Premium Features" icon="star">
    Additional revenue from advanced agent capabilities, priority access, or private plugins channels back to the staking pool.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Market Expansion" icon="chart-network">
    New revenue streams emerge as Sentio integrates with additional blockchains or protocols, directly benefiting the stake reward pool.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
