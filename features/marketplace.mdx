## Monetization Models

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card
    title="Usage Fees"
    icon="money-bill"
  >
    Users pay a small fee whenever the agent executes a transaction.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Subscription Plans"
    icon="calendar"
  >
    Agents offering ongoing services can charge monthly or annual rates.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Revenue Sharing"
    icon="chart-pie"
  >
    A portion of the agent's generated profits is automatically allocated to the creator.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Incentives for Adoption

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Creator Royalties">
    Guaranteed earnings on every transaction executed by their agent, creating a sustainable income stream for innovative creators.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Referral Programs">
    Users or influencers that onboard new members can receive a share of fees, encouraging community growth.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Featured Listings">
    High-performing agents are showcased in "Featured" or "Top Rated" sections to drive visibility and reward excellence.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
